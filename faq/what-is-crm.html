What is a CRM System?

A CRM (Customer Relationship Management) system centralises all customer data -- contact details, purchase history, communication logs, and support records -- into a single platform. It enables sales, marketing, and service teams to work from the same information, improving coordination and client outcomes.

Example: When a sales representative logs a client conversation, that record is immediately available to support and account management teams. This eliminates information silos and ensures consistent communication.

AZ Khonnect implements CRM platforms tailored to your team's workflow -- both custom-built and Odoo-based -- to improve client retention, service quality, and pipeline visibility.
