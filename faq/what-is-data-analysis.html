What is Data Analysis?

Data analysis is the systematic process of examining data to extract actionable insights. It enables organisations to identify trends, measure performance, and make evidence-based decisions rather than relying on intuition.

Example: A retail company analysing daily sales data can identify seasonal demand patterns, underperforming locations, and the true ROI of marketing campaigns -- insights that directly inform strategy.

The discipline encompasses data cleaning, statistical modelling, regression analysis, and machine learning -- with the right technique selected based on the data type and business question at hand.

AZ Khonnect provides end-to-end data analysis services, from data structuring and warehousing through to insight delivery and dashboard reporting.
