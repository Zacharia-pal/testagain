Formation 3.0 Accounting & Management

Client: Formation 3.0 ASBL — Belgium
Role: IT Analyst & Project Manager
Period: October 2022 – June 2025 (2+ years)

Overview and Challenge

Formation 3.0 ASBL is a Belgian non-profit organization that needed a modern, data-driven approach to its accounting and management operations. As the organization grew, its financial processes required more sophisticated tooling and reporting capabilities. There was also a strategic need to identify and secure new funding opportunities, including EU-funded programs such as Erasmus+, to sustain and expand the organization's mission. The challenge was to build a comprehensive management infrastructure that could scale with the organization while maintaining full financial transparency.

Our Approach

We took full ownership of the accounting systems and built a suite of custom Python analytics tools to provide real-time insight into the organization's financial health. Power BI dashboards were developed to give stakeholders clear, interactive visualizations of key financial metrics, project budgets, and operational performance indicators. These dashboards replaced static reports and enabled faster, more informed decision-making at every level of the organization.

Beyond accounting, we actively sourced new projects and funding opportunities. This included preparing and managing applications for Erasmus+ EU funding programs, coordinating project deliverables, and ensuring compliance with grant requirements. Custom software solutions were developed to address specific operational needs, and a Human Resource Information System (HRIS) was implemented and managed to streamline personnel administration.

Technologies Used

  • Python (custom analytics tools and automation scripts)
  • Power BI (interactive dashboards and financial reporting)
  • HRIS (human resource information management)
  • Custom software development for operational needs

Key Deliverables

  • End-to-end accounting system management
  • Custom Python-based analytics and reporting tools
  • Interactive Power BI dashboards for financial and operational KPIs
  • Erasmus+ project sourcing, application, and management
  • Custom software solutions tailored to organizational needs
  • HRIS implementation and ongoing management

Results and Impact

The project transformed Formation 3.0's approach to financial management and organizational analytics. Decision-makers gained access to real-time data through intuitive dashboards, replacing guesswork with evidence-based planning. The successful acquisition of Erasmus+ funding opened new avenues for the organization's programs. The custom software and HRIS solutions reduced administrative overhead and improved operational efficiency across the board. The sustained impact of this work over more than two years contributed directly to the organization's growth and operational maturity.
