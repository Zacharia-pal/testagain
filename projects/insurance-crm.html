Insurance CRM Platform — Patronale Life

Client: Patronale Life NV — Brussels, Belgium
Role: Business Analyst, Product Owner, Developer, Functional Analyst
Period: March 2025 – Present

Overview and Challenge

Patronale Life NV, a Belgian insurance company headquartered in Brussels, operates a complex ecosystem of business systems to support its mortgage broker operations. The organization relied on multiple platforms — Odoo (ERP), AFAS (financial and HR), and PolApp (broker platform) — that needed to work together seamlessly. The challenge was to implement a CRM solution within this multi-system landscape that could unify broker workflows, eliminate data silos, and provide a single source of truth for customer relationship management across the insurance value chain.

Our Approach

We began with extensive requirements gathering sessions with stakeholders across departments, mapping out existing workflows and identifying pain points in the broker management process. Functional documentation was produced to capture the current state and define the target architecture. As Product Owner, we prioritized the backlog to ensure the highest-impact features were delivered first, coordinating sprints through Jira and maintaining comprehensive documentation in Confluence.

The core of the project involved deep customization of the Odoo ERP platform to align with insurance broker workflows. This included custom modules, tailored views, and automated business logic specific to the insurance domain. We conducted thorough analysis of the PolApp broker platform to understand integration points and data flows. Cross-system optimization between Odoo, AFAS, and PolApp was designed and implemented to ensure data consistency, reduce duplicate entry, and streamline operations across all three platforms.

Technologies Used

  • Odoo ERP (custom modules, views, and business logic)
  • Python and XML (Odoo development)
  • Jinja (templating)
  • AFAS (financial and HR system integration)
  • PolApp (broker platform analysis and integration)
  • Jira and Confluence (project management and documentation)
  • Git (version control)

Key Deliverables

  • Odoo ERP customizations tailored to insurance broker workflows
  • Cross-system optimization between Odoo, AFAS, and PolApp
  • Functional analysis and documentation of broker processes
  • Stakeholder alignment and requirements specification
  • Backlog prioritization and sprint coordination
  • PolApp broker platform analysis and integration design

Results and Impact

The CRM platform implementation brought structure and visibility to Patronale Life's broker management operations. By optimizing the interplay between Odoo, AFAS, and PolApp, the project eliminated redundant data entry and reduced the risk of cross-system inconsistencies. Broker workflows became more efficient, with clear process definitions and automated handoffs between systems. The Jira-managed delivery process ensured predictable, incremental value delivery while maintaining alignment with business priorities across the organization.
