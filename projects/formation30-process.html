Process Optimization — Formation 3.0

Client: Formation 3.0 ASBL — Belgium
Role: IT Analyst & Project Manager, later Non-Executive Board Member
Period: October 2022 – June 2025

Overview and Challenge

Formation 3.0 ASBL, a Belgian non-profit, faced the challenge common to many growing organizations: processes that worked at a smaller scale were becoming bottlenecks as the organization expanded. IT systems were underutilized, manual workflows persisted where automation was possible, and the organization lacked a structured approach to identifying and securing new funding opportunities. There was a clear need for someone who could analyze existing processes, introduce modern tooling, and drive organizational improvement from both a technical and strategic perspective.

Our Approach

We conducted a comprehensive analysis of the organization's processes, identifying areas where technology could reduce manual effort and improve outcomes. Custom Python tools were developed to automate repetitive tasks and provide analytics capabilities that the organization previously lacked. These tools were designed to be maintainable and practical, fitting the resource constraints of a non-profit environment.

A significant part of the role involved sourcing and managing EU-funded projects, particularly through the Erasmus+ program. This required understanding the funding landscape, preparing compelling applications, and managing the administrative and reporting requirements once funding was secured. Each project was managed end-to-end, from initial opportunity identification through to final reporting and close-out.

We also developed custom software solutions tailored to the organization's specific needs and implemented an HRIS to bring structure to personnel management. The combination of process analysis, custom development, and strategic project management created a comprehensive improvement program that touched every part of the organization's operations.

Technologies Used

  • Python (custom analytics and automation tools)
  • HRIS (human resource information management)
  • Custom software development
  • Erasmus+ project management frameworks

Key Deliverables

  • Organization-wide process analysis and improvement roadmap
  • Custom Python analytics and automation tools
  • Erasmus+ project sourcing, application, and management
  • Custom software solutions for operational needs
  • HRIS implementation and management
  • Strategic recommendations as Non-Executive Board Member

Results and Impact

The sustained process optimization work transformed Formation 3.0's operational capabilities. Manual workflows were replaced with automated solutions, freeing staff to focus on the organization's core mission. The successful acquisition and management of Erasmus+ projects brought new funding and expanded the organization's reach. Custom software addressed specific operational gaps that off-the-shelf solutions could not fill. The impact of this work was recognized by the organization's leadership, resulting in an elevation to the role of Non-Executive Board Member, where strategic guidance continued to shape the organization's direction.
