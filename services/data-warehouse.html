Data Warehousing & Analytics

Sound decisions require reliable data. At AZ Khonnect, we build centralised data warehouses that consolidate information from every corner of your organisation into a single, query-ready repository. Combined with automated pipelines and modern visualisation tools, we give your leadership team the reporting foundation they need to act with confidence.

Our Approach

We follow an ELT (Extract, Load, Transform) architecture that prioritises flexibility and scalability. Raw data is extracted from your source systems—ERP platforms, CRM databases, spreadsheets, APIs—and loaded into a PostgreSQL data warehouse hosted on Linux infrastructure. Transformations are then applied within the warehouse itself, making it straightforward to adapt models as your reporting needs evolve.

Pipeline orchestration is managed through Apache Airflow. We design, develop, and deploy Directed Acyclic Graphs (DAGs) that schedule and monitor every stage of your data flow—from extraction through to final reporting tables. Airflow provides full visibility into pipeline health, with alerting for failures and automatic retries to ensure data freshness.

On the reporting side, we connect your warehouse to Power BI or custom dashboards, delivering interactive visualisations that surface the metrics that matter most. Whether you need daily operational reports or strategic quarterly summaries, the data is always current and trustworthy.

Key Capabilities

  • PostgreSQL Data Warehouses – Schema design, indexing strategies, partitioning, and performance tuning on Linux-based infrastructure (including AlmaLinux environments).
  • Apache Airflow – DAG development, scheduling configuration, custom operators, connection management, and monitoring for reliable pipeline orchestration.
  • ELT Architecture – Design and implementation of extract-load-transform workflows that keep raw data accessible while delivering clean, modelled datasets for analysis.
  • Power BI Dashboards – Interactive reports, KPI scorecards, and drill-down analytics connected directly to your warehouse via live or scheduled refresh.
  • Data Modelling – Dimensional modelling, star schemas, and business-logic layers that translate raw records into meaningful, analyst-friendly structures.
  • Cross-System Integration – Data extraction from Odoo, AFAS, REST APIs, flat files, and legacy databases, with reconciliation checks to ensure consistency.
  • SQL Development – Complex query authoring, stored procedures, views, and materialised tables optimised for reporting performance.
