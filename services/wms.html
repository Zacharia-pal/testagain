Warehouse Management Systems

In logistics, visibility is everything. AZ Khonnect designs and implements warehouse management systems that give you real-time control over inventory, operations, and workforce deployment. Drawing on hands-on experience in rapid-delivery logistics—including operations at Gorillas—we understand the demands of environments where speed, accuracy, and coordination cannot be compromised.

Our Approach

We work with your operations team to understand the physical flow of goods through your warehouse or distribution centre: receiving, storage, picking, packing, and dispatch. From that foundation, we design a system that tracks every item in real time, automates routine decisions, and surfaces the operational data your managers need to keep things running efficiently.

For organisations with delivery fleets, we extend this into rider and driver operations management—covering scheduling, route assignment, and real-time status tracking. The goal is a single operational view that connects warehouse activity with last-mile delivery, eliminating information gaps between teams.

We also focus on the analytics side. Operational statistics—order fulfilment rates, pick accuracy, delivery times, workforce utilisation—are captured automatically and presented through dashboards that support both daily management and strategic planning.

Key Capabilities

  • Real-Time Inventory Tracking – Live visibility into stock levels, locations, and movements across one or multiple warehouse sites.
  • Rider & Driver Operations – Scheduling, dispatch, and performance tracking for delivery teams, built for high-volume, time-sensitive environments.
  • Workforce Planning – Shift scheduling, capacity forecasting, and labour allocation tools that match staffing to demand.
  • Operational Analytics – Automated reporting on fulfilment rates, cycle times, error rates, and productivity metrics via Power BI or custom dashboards.
  • Supply Chain Integration – Connections to ERP systems (Odoo, AFAS), supplier portals, and transport management platforms for end-to-end visibility.
  • Custom Development – Purpose-built modules using Python and PostgreSQL when off-the-shelf WMS solutions do not fit your operational model.

What We Deliver

  • A warehouse management platform configured for your specific operational workflows
  • Real-time dashboards for inventory status, order progress, and delivery tracking
  • Integration with your existing ERP and supply chain systems
  • Workforce scheduling and performance management tools
  • Operator training and process documentation
  • Ongoing support and iterative improvement based on operational data

Who This Is For

Our WMS services are built for logistics operators, distribution centres, e-commerce fulfilment teams, and any organisation that manages physical inventory at scale. We bring particular expertise to rapid-delivery and last-mile logistics, where operational margins are tight and every minute matters. Whether you are running a single warehouse or coordinating across multiple sites, we deliver systems that keep your operations visible, measurable, and under control.
