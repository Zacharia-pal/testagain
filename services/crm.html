CRM Implementation

A well-implemented CRM is the backbone of effective client relationships. At AZ Khonnect, we deploy and customise CRM platforms that give your sales, marketing, and account management teams a single source of truth—from first contact through to long-term retention. We do not simply install software; we configure it around the way your teams actually work, ensuring high adoption and measurable impact on revenue.

Our Approach

We start by auditing your current client management processes: how leads are captured, how opportunities are tracked, and where deals fall through the cracks. Based on that analysis, we design a CRM architecture that fits your sales cycle, whether that involves a straightforward pipeline or a complex multi-stage process with approvals and handoffs.

Data migration is handled with precision. We extract, clean, and transform records from legacy systems—spreadsheets, old databases, or competing CRM platforms—and load them into your new environment with full integrity checks. No client history is lost in the transition.

Once the platform is live, we invest heavily in user training and change management. A CRM only delivers value if people use it consistently. We provide hands-on workshops, documentation, and ongoing support to ensure your team is confident from day one.

Technologies & Platforms

  • Odoo CRM – Full deployment, customisation, and module development for Odoo's integrated CRM suite, including lead scoring, pipeline automation, and reporting dashboards.
  • Custom CRM Solutions – For organisations with unique requirements, we build tailored CRM applications using Python and PostgreSQL, with web-based interfaces designed for your specific workflows.
  • AFAS Integration – Bi-directional synchronisation between your CRM and AFAS ERP, ensuring that client data, invoicing, and contract management stay aligned across platforms.
  • Cross-Platform Connectivity – Integration with third-party tools such as PolApp, email marketing platforms, and accounting software to create a unified client management ecosystem.
  • Power BI Reporting – Sales performance dashboards, conversion analytics, and pipeline forecasting connected directly to your CRM data.

What We Deliver

  • Lead capture and qualification workflows tailored to your sales process
  • Automated pipeline management with stage-based triggers and notifications
  • Client retention tools including follow-up scheduling and satisfaction tracking
  • Complete data migration from legacy systems with validation and deduplication
  • Role-based access controls and security configuration
  • User training programmes and adoption support

Who This Is For

Our CRM implementation services are designed for businesses that need more than a contact list. Whether you are a growing company deploying your first CRM, or an established organisation replacing a legacy system that no longer scales, we deliver a platform that drives pipeline visibility and client engagement. We have particular experience serving insurance firms, HR service providers, non-profits managing donor relationships, and hospitality businesses tracking guest interactions and bookings.
