About Us

A trusted Belgian IT partner delivering measurable business outcomes.

AZ Khonnect is a Belgium-founded IT solutions and consulting firm specialising in enterprise systems that drive operational efficiency. We design, build, and implement technology platforms -- from CRM and ERP to data warehouses and custom software -- that enable organisations to make better decisions and scale with confidence. Sustainability, both ecological and economic, is embedded in every engagement.

Industry Expertise

We bring proven experience across sectors where operational complexity demands robust IT infrastructure:

  • HR & Recruitment: Workflow automation for talent acquisition, onboarding, and retention management.
  • Logistics: Real-time inventory tracking, route optimisation, and warehouse management systems.
  • Finance: Secure reporting platforms, budget management, and financial performance dashboards.
  • Hospitality: Integrated reservation, order management, and guest engagement platforms for hotels and restaurants.

Every engagement is scoped to the client's specific requirements. We tailor our approach whether working with growing businesses or established enterprises.

Why AZ Khonnect

  • Domain Expertise: Deep understanding of the industries we serve, not just the technology.
  • Full-Lifecycle Delivery: From strategic analysis and architecture through implementation, training, and support.
  • Sustainable by Design: Resource-efficient solutions that minimise waste and maximise long-term value.
  • Belgian Roots, International Reach: Headquartered in Belgium with the capacity to serve clients across borders.

We deliver clarity, structure, and measurable improvement. Our work is defined by outcomes, not promises.
